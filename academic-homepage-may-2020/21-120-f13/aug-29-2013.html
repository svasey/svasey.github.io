Recitation of Aug. 29, 2013

We discussed the exponential function, and its behavior as x goes to plus and minus infinity. Recall that this depends on the value of the base, namely on whether it is larger or less than one.

We computed some limits: the first thing to try is usually to plug in the value x goes to inside the expression, and see if we can get more information that way. If this does not work (and usually, it won't), there are various tricks to try: we saw two of them:

  • Factoring the polynomials involved.
  • (with the 11h30 recitation) Multiplying by the conjugate over itself.
We will see many more tricks and methods in the coming weeks !
